Paylio now gives you a dedicated bank account number

Our goal is to implement the easiest ways of transacting. We have released a new feature that lets you fund your Paylio account / wallet swiftly which is the dedicated account number.

Your paylio account number belongs to you alone and carries your full name. This means any funds transferred to the account number will automatically reflect on your Paylio wallet.

 

How to get your account number.

All verified users  on Paylio automatically have an account number allocated to them.

Click on “Fund wallet” to view your account number.

 

How to verify your paylio account.

The dedicated account number is also equivalent to a bank account number. In order to get this account generated, You need to get your paylio details correctly verified which also require you to link your “BVN” to protect you from fraud.

What does this mean for You?

  • You can now fund your paylio wallet directly with internet banking, USSD and other bank transfer channels (without the need for a debit card)
  • You can accept transfers from friends and family directly into your wallet.
  • You can receive payments for services straight into your Paylio wallet.

Why use this ?

Transferring to your paylio account number is the fastest way of funding your wallet.

  1. No creating of transaction log
  2. No awaiting approval from operators. This means our operators do not need to confirm and approve your payment before it reflects on your paylio wallet.
  3. Automatic/instant notification on funding of wallet. Once your transfer is successfully sent to your Paylio account number. You will be notified of the transfer received on your mail / transaction history.

 

You can receive money from friends and family and also get paid for your services directly into your Paylio account.
