What is Nairapin?

Nairapin is a simple way to send and receive money, reward your friends, fans and followers with a simple pin that they can redeem into cash and get it in their bank account in less than 2 minutes.
A very nice feature for rewarding giveaway winners or paying fans online. Give it a try, you will love it.

How to Generate Nairapin?

  • Log in to your paylio account via the Paylio Wallet App
  • Click on "Nairapin" and select "Generate Nairapin"
  • Input the Amount and Number of units you want to generate.
Note:
  • You need to have sufficient funds in your wallet to generate nairapin
  • The issued Nairapin can instantly be redeemed via the Nairapin link or using the Paylio Wallet app
